My Favorite Tourist Attraction

Dollywood

Dollywood is located in Pigeon Forge, Tennessee. I am thankful to say that I have been able to travel to Dollywood many times! It is an amusement park founded by a country singer named Dolly Parton.

Dollywood has so much to offer! It not only has amusement rides, but also a train, restaurants to eat at, shops to visit, and live shows to watch. One of my favorite things to do is ride on the train located in the park! For more information on my favorite tourist attraction, please visit: https://www.dollywood.com

Reasons Why I Love Dollywood

  1. Train Ride
  2. Other Fun Rides
  3. Mini Shows
  4. Live Shows
