My Favorite Season

Fall

I love fall because the weather starts to get cooler and is more comfortable to me. If you get a little chilly, you can even put on a cozy sweater!

I also like going to pumpkin patches during this time because it is so beautiful outside. It's nice to do activities outside in Fall before Winter comes. I love seeing the pretty colors when the leaves start to change as well. Taking a nice walk or car ride to see the changes is something fun I like to do. The only sad part about Fall is that it does not last long, so I try to remember to enjoy it! Please visit: https://www.travelwisconsin.com to learn more about possible places to travel to in Wisconsin to see the leaves change their color.

Why do I love Fall?

  • Changing leaf colors
  • Halloween!
  • Comfortable weather
  • Pumpkin Patches
